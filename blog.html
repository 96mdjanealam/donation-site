Important FAQ ?

How can I make a donation?

You can donate via our website using secure payment options, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, or bank transfers.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, donations are typically tax-deductible. Please consult with a tax advisor for specific details related to your donation.

Can I specify how my donation is used?

Absolutely! You can designate your donation to specific programs or allow us to allocate it where it's most needed.

Is my personal information secure when I donate?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and payment information during the donation process.

Can I set up recurring donations?

Yes, you can choose to make recurring donations on a monthly, quarterly, or annual basis for continued support.
